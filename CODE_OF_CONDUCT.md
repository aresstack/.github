# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not individuals.
- Accept feedback professionally.
- Help keep discussions inclusive and productive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insults, or inflammatory language
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content and contributions that violate this code of conduct.

For concerns, contact maintainers through repository support or security channels as appropriate.
